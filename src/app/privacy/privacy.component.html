Privacy Policy

Team GymRatTrax built the GymRatTrax app as a free and open source app as a school project and is continued to be maintained as a hobby. The app is provided by Team GymRatTrax at no cost and is intended for use as is. It will never collect or store any information from the user, it will never display ads for the user, and it will never charge for any use of the app. It is maintained as a personal individual project and is not represented by any business entity.

Team GymRatTrax does not collect or store any information. While the application does ask for personal information such as birth date, this information is stored locally on the device for the purpose of calculations, such as estimates for how many calories will be burned by engaging a given activity. No analytics data is recorded or retrieved by Team GymRatTrax.

The application does give the user the option of using Google Play Services for the following optional features:

  • Synchronizing activities completed in the app with one's own Google Fit history
  • Adding activities to one's own Google Calendar
  • Storing a backup of settings to one's own Google Account

The user is always given an option to engage or to not engage in these features. Use of these features is guided by the Google Play Services privacy policy.

For a better experience, while using the app, it may require you to provide certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to birth date. The information requested will be retained on your device and is not collected in any way.

Log Data

In a case of an error while using the app, some data and information may be sent via Google Play in order to diagnose potential issues. This information may include information such as the device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing the app, the time and date of your use of the app, and other statistics.

Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. These are sent to your browser from the websites that you visit and are stored on your device's internal memory.

This app does not use these “cookies” explicitly. However, the app may use third-party code and libraries that use “cookies” to collect information and improve their services. You have the option to either accept or refuse these cookies and know when a cookie is being sent to your device. If you choose to refuse our cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of this app.

Security

This personal project does not intentionally collect any data from the user and the source code will remain open and available. No guarantee is made over the absolute security over how the local device data is stored or whether the use of integrations such as Google Play Services is over an up-to-date and safe protocol.

Links to Other Sites

This app may contain links to other sites. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. Note that these external sites are not operated by Team GymRatTrax. Therefore, it is strongly advised that you review the Privacy Policy of these websites. Team GymRatTrax have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes will be made available through this page.
